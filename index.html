Our Approach

Step by Step

NumberTex focuses on high‑conversion, low‑friction trade intermediation between buyers and suppliers in selected B2B niches. The process starts with clear objectives and ends with a structured path from lead to conversation. By focusing on qualified opportunities and removing unnecessary intermediaries, the model delivers maximum conversion for export and sourcing opportunities.

The first step is to build a clear map of the target market, identifying real buyers and suppliers that match product, price, and logistics. Once the right profiles are identified, they are qualified on volume, payment terms, Incoterms, and basic fit before any formal introduction.

After validation, the focus on maximum conversion begins. The introduction is made with clear, documented terms, and the follow‑up keeps the commercial discussion moving forward. There are no unnecessary layers and no over‑promising. The result is a lean, direct, and practical path from first contact to real B2B trade.

This approach is particularly effective for companies in export, sourcing, industrial materials, and commodities, where fast, accurate intermediation significantly increases the chance of closing a deal.
